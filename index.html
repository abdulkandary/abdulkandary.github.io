ABOUT ABDULLAH

I've spent the majority of my adult life navigating my passions in the arts and the sciences. I began my research journey with materials sciences back in 2015 and developed a keen passion on exploring the field ever since. My natural meditative state peaks when relating to another fellow biological being; when contemplating the fact that my ceramic- and fiber-reinforced polymer and lipid matrix, i.e., body, undergoes the same biochemical reactions. My research objective is to engineer natural polymer composites, priorotizing mechanics and fabrication at present, with tailored functional properties on the long-run.

Besides the natural sciences, there is no element more integral to my human experience than music. I sing and play the oud (ʿūd). Whether harmonizing with choirs or jamming in the traditional Kuwaiti setting (samrat), with Eastern or Western bands, I immerse myself in music wherever I tread. Currently focusing on Arab music theory and advancing my oud technique.
